Active Recall Method

    Ingredients:
  • Study materials (textbooks, notes, flashcards)
  • Timer or stopwatch
  • Quiet study environment
    Instructions:
  1. Start by selecting a specific topic or concept you want to study.
  2. Set a timer for a predetermined amount of time(e.g.,20 minutes)
  3. Begin studying the material actively by reading, summarizing, or taking notes.
  4. Once the timer goes off, put away the study materials.
  5. Without looking at the materials, try to recall and explain the information you just studied in your own words
  6. Use the study materials to check your accuracy and identify areas of weakness.
  7. Repeat the process with different topics or concepts.
  8. Gradually increase the study duration as you become more comfortable with the material.
  9. Review the areas that require more attention and continue practicing active recall regularly for effective learning.
