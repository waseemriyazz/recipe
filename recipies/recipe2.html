Pomodoro Technique

    Ingredients:
  • Study materials (textbooks, notes, flashcards)
  • Timer or stopwatch
  • Notebook or paper for jotting down distractions
    Instructions:
  1. Set a timer for 25 minutes (this is known as one "Pomodoro").
  2. Focus solely on studying for the entire duration without any distractions.
  3. If distractions or unrelated thoughts arise, jot them down on a separate piece of paper without engaging with them.
  4. Once the timer goes off, take a short 5-minute break.
  5. During the break, stretch, move around, or do something enjoyable and unrelated to studying.
  6. After the break, start another Pomodoro by setting the timer for 25 minutes and repeating the focused study session.
  7. After completing four Pomodoros, take a longer break of around 15-30 minutes.
  8. Repeat this cycle of focused studying and short breaks until you've covered your study goals for the session.
  9. The Pomodoro Technique helps to improve concentration, manage time effectively, and maintain productivity during study sessions.
